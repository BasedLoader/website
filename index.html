What the modding community deserves.

The Minecraft modding community has had to deal with constant compromises, no matter what mod loader they use. We can do better than that.

Our work merging Forge & Fabric

We have been working hard to remove the barrier between the two main competing mod loaders. Currently, support is very limited for some fabric mods due to mixin issues and Forge's mod class loading being broken, but is a big step in the direction towards one mod loader for all mods.

Plans to extend the api

We plan to have an api with fabrics modern style combined with some upcoming features such as extended interface injection and to meet or exceed the scope of forges API.
